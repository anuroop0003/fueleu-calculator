About This Tool

The CII Maritime Calculator supports vessel carbon intensity performance review and rating checks through a clear, professional interface. It includes the IMO CII Calculator for standard CII rating output, the Canadian CII Calculator for Transport Canada compliant ratings, and the IMO DCS & CII Verification Tool for vessel data collection and carbon intensity indicator checks.
