About This Tool

This platform brings together a suite of maritime decarbonization & performance tools for ships — covering vessel stability and hydrostatics, IMO CII & EEXI compliance, and FuelEU Maritime GHG intensity tracking. It's built to help naval architects, ship operators and technical superintendents quickly assess carbon intensity, fuel efficiency and regulatory compliance in one place.
